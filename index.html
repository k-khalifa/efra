Welcome to the Annual EFRE Conference

Egyptian Foundation Of Reproductive Medicine and Embryology

On behalf of the Egyptian Foundation for Reproductive Medicine and Embryology (EFRE), it is a great pleasure to welcome you all to 2st Annual Scientific Conference & Expo EFRE 2022 in the historically rich city of Cairo.

The Scientific Congress & Expo focus on the many innovations in reproductive medicine with a comprehensive and provocative program for Congress attendees. The entire program is designed to address the educational and practice needs of reproductive specialists around the globe.

The symposia and interactive sessions will touch on a wide range of topics from medical therapies for reproductive medicine to cutting-edge research on reproductive biology and surgery.

I welcome our members, trainees and colleagues from around the world to the first EFRE 2022 scientific Congress & Expo. We value your participation and engagement and hope you will take advantage of the opportunities provided by the meeting to interact and network with colleagues.

I look forward to seeing you in the exciting city of Cairo from June 01-03, 2022 at the unique Hilton Cairo Heliopolis. I also encourage all of you to join our recently founded foundation and support the research efforts to further improve standards of practicing reproductive medicine and embryology.1
